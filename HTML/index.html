Introduction

Welcome to the ultimate resource for 'The Circle' by Dave Eggers, a novel that critically examines the digital age's impact on privacy, democracy, and personal freedom. Here, we offer an in-depth look at the plot, characters, and the provocative themes Eggers presents through his narrative. Whether you're a first-time reader or revisiting the story, this site serves as your guide to understanding the complex questions the book raises about our increasingly connected world. 'The Circle' invites readers to reflect on the role of technology in our lives, challenging us to consider how the digital world shapes our identities, relationships, and societal structures.
